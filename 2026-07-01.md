## SARDINE pipeline (reference)

Wio Terminal chess engine — **≥ 2000 Elo**, **~1 s/move**, **192 KB RAM**.

1. Feature encoder — pruned **716** features (pawn-rank zeros, king mirroring, castling, en passant)
2. Search skeleton on PC (C++) — perft, eval hooks, TT format benchmark
3. Train bucketed NNUE — `716 → 16 → 1`, 8 queen-split buckets; Lc0 data; nnue-pytorch → int8
4. Queen-split ablation — per-bucket MSE vs piece-count baseline
5. Incremental accumulators on device
6. Port search + NNUE to C — benchmark `-O3` vs `-Os`
7. Full search stack — quiescence, futility, LMR, null-move, lazy eval, ID, SPSA
8. **Elo gate** — iterate or proceed to v2 (policy head, UCI polish)
